Welcome to new KRUU app!

This is a simple app that will connect job seekers with positions that need filling. It will help managers get qualified employees for job openings without the hassle of email this can all be done in the palm of your hand on the app.

The app will also allow job seekers create a resume with the ability to and save or download it for later. How do we monetize? When managers need an immediate filling they can bump up the priority of the post by paying for advertising.
